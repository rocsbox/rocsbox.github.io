Expectations Assignment

For this assignment, we were asked to pick a genre and corresponding movie with the objective being to redesign the poster in such a way that altered ones opinion of what genre said movie belonged to. For my assignment, I chose the movie Baby Driver. Baby driver is an action film that depicts a young musically gifted getaway driver seeking to escape a life of crime. It did not take me long to come up with a new name for the movie, Leggo Driver. From the creators of Leggo, a newfound racing adventure full of friends, frenemies, and epic musical performances. In my work-in-progress 2 reflection, I will go over the details of the poster transformation. Below you can find the original, first edit, and final revision of this activity set.
